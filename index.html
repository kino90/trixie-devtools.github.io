Modular Developer tools for modern web applications

Modular and extensible

Every team has its own setup, so everyone need different tools.
With Trixie you can download and plug just the modules you need and build yourself a tool that covers your whole workflow!

One size fits all

Check your server status, manage your PHP sessions, add or remove gems to your Ruby applications, handle your databases and so on, the possibilities are endless!

Open for everyone

Just plug in the modules you need from github or our store and you're ready to go.
Don't find a module that suit your needs? Don't panic, you can write your own module and even share it with our community of developers! Easy as A-B-C!
